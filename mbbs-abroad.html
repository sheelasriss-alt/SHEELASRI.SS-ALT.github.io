FAQs About MBBS Abroad

Find answers to common questions about studying medicine in other countries.

Is NEET required for MBBS abroad?

Yes, as per NMC regulations, a valid NEET score is mandatory for Indian students to pursue MBBS abroad.

Which countries are best for MBBS?

Russia, Ukraine, China, and the Philippines are popular choices due to quality education and affordable fees.

What are the eligibility criteria?

Generally, you need to have a science background with a minimum percentage in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology and a valid NEET score.

Can I get a loan for my studies?

Yes, many banks in India offer education loans for MBBS programs abroad.
