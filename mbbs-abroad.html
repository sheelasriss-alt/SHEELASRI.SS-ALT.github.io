BMTU Medical College

Vietnam

The MBBS program at BMTU Medical College is a comprehensive 6-year course, including a 1-year internship, designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful medical career.

Eligibility Requirements

Applicants must have completed their 10+2 education with a strong background in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, achieving the minimum required scores.

Admission Process

The admission process typically involves an online application, submission of academic transcripts, a personal statement, and letters of recommendation. Our team can assist with the entire process.
